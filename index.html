About

At Focus Over Fear Apparel, our mission is to empower individuals to embrace their dreams and live boldly, free from the constraints of fear. We create high-quality, inspiring clothing that serves as a reminder to prioritize passion and purpose over doubt and hesitation. Through our designs, we aim to ignite a spirit of courage to seize each moment. Together, we celebrate resilience, creativity, and the unwavering belief that when we focus on our dreams, we can overcome any obstacle.

Several of the products we use for our apparel include the following:

  • Bella+Canvas 3001
  • Gildan 64000
  • Gildan 18000
  • Gildan 18500
  • Scented Soy Candles
We also offer Standard, Economy and Express Shipping on select items and are US based company.
